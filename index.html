About Me

My name is Christopher Cavazos, and if you want to learn more about me, you’ve come to the right place. To start I am a new developer, working through the University of Washington’s code academy to kick-start my development career. I’ve had a fascination for technology almost as long as I could breathe. Whether it was tinkering with my old Sega Genesis to see if I could put it back together, or my “might-as-well-be-my-second-job” status whenever my close friends or family have any tech related issues.


Besides technology, sports have been the number one passion in my life. It was my dream to play on a professional sports team for the city of Seattle, but alas, with injuries and many set-backs that had to go to the wayside. After an entire pandemic of soul-searching, I realized tech was the industry I could pursue my new dreams developing by creating new applications and problem solving code. I like to relate coding to playing a popular game known for its head-smashing difficulty and frustration… Dark Souls. Every new project is just a Dark Souls boss, with new and old mechanics that always take you for a loop. Some come easy and others are Like Ornstein and Smough, there are no shortcuts, it’s just hard work, problem solving, and even a little bit of luck. But once that newest challenge is bested, whether it’s boss or project, there is no rival feeling of satisfaction compared to “I did it”. That is the feeling I long for in my new career in coding, to know that through my hard work, I can succeed.
